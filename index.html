The History Of Our Hotel

Supreme Aegis Hotel has been open since 1980, many people have traveled around the world to come and check out our hotel for decades. We started as a very small hotel but throughout the many years we've been open, we have upgraded our building to look more beautiful and attract people. Our staff provides excellent services and has been for years now. Our number 1 priority is to make sure all of our customers are happy and satisfied when they arrive and when they leave.
